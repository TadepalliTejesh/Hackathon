What is PACE?

The Plankton, Aerosol, Cloud, ocean Ecosystem (PACE) mission is helping scientists study the Earth's oceans, atmosphere, and their impact on climate. With data on ocean color, aerosols, and clouds, PACE provides crucial insights into climate change and environmental health.

Our platform brings this fascinating science into classrooms, helping students of all ages explore the wonders of Earth science.
